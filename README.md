## Power BI Sales & Profit Dashboard

### Project Overview
This repository showcases a **Power BI dashboard (PDF export)** created as part of a hands-on learning exercise.  
The dashboard analyzes **sales, profit, discounts, and order performance** across regions, categories, and customer segments using a Superstore-style dataset.

The purpose of this mini-project was to understand **how to design business-focused dashboards** and interpret insights using Power BI visuals.

---

### Business Questions Explored
- How do sales and profit vary across regions?
- Which customer segments contribute most to revenue?
- How do product categories perform over time?
- Where are profit losses occurring despite strong sales?
- How does quantity impact profitability?

---

### Dashboard Contents

**Key KPIs**
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Discount  
- Quantity Sold  
- Number of Orders  

**Visuals Included**
- Sales by Region  
- Profit by Quantity  
- Sales by Segment  
- Sales & Profit by Year and Category  
- Profit by Category  
- Region, Segment, Category, and Date filters
